## About

I’m **gomezdj**, a **precision bioinformatician and clinical investigator** working at the intersection of genomics, translational research, and patient-centered clinical studies. My work focuses on building analytically rigorous, reproducible methods that turn complex molecular and clinical data into evidence that can inform real-world decisions.

### What I do
- **Precision genomics & multi-omics analysis:** variant interpretation, cohort-scale analyses, and clinically relevant biomarker discovery
- **Clinical/translational investigation:** study design support, endpoint definitions, and aligning analyses with clinical context and regulatory-grade expectations
- **Reproducible computational science:** workflow development, versioned analysis, documentation, and transparent reporting

### How I work
I care deeply about **data provenance, quality control, and interpretability**—especially where analyses may influence clinical hypotheses, downstream validation, or patient care. I aim to build pipelines and analyses that are:
- **Reproducible** (automated, versioned, well-documented)
- **Auditable** (clear inputs/outputs, traceable decisions)
- **Clinically aware** (built around real constraints: samples, phenotypes, bias, missingness)
